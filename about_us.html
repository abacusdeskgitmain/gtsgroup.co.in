About Us

 

Who We Are

 

Located in Faridabad, Delhi-NCR, Global Technical Solutions, is an ISO 9001:2008 Certified Company that supplies full-service Injection mold design & development, Precision mold tooling, Custom injection molding, and Integrated packaging & forwarding to the Automotive, Appliance, Industrial Tools and Medical Industries.

 

GTS specializes in precision, high volume production molds & production quality prototypes; custom molded plastics and integrated assembly. From co-development of injection molded components through production, assembly and packaging of molded plastics, ,GTS is committed to providing responsive solution driven injection molding services.

 

 

The values of GTS’s services include:

 

Speed-to-Market service through in-house design, development and CAD to CAM tooling and machining capability operating 24 hours a day,

 

Maximum Efficiency through optimization of mold performance with Mold flow analysis, Melt Management Technologies and Shot scope monitoring systems,

 

Increased Product Performance through design & development collaboration, lean production methods, scientific molding and high-performance polymer processing,

 

Responsive Customer Service through dedicated program management that corresponds and communicates with you through all stages of product manufacturing,

 

Highest Total Delivered Value through strategic project management control from design to delivery and throughout the entire product life cycle.
