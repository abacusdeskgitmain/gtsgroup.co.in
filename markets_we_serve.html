AUTOMOTIVE MARKET

GTS provides Molds and Molding Solutions to the Automotive Market, supporting customers from design concept to full production. We provide customers with product design, material development, prototyping, and launch into production for true project management of a new product.

 

 

 

APPLIANCE MARKET

GTS provides Molds and Molding Solutions of various plastic components used by a number of different Household Appliances manufacturers particularly OEM’s.

 

 

 

 

 

MEDICAL MARKET

GTS by following systems approach capture the medical injection molding market and provide Molds and Molding Solutions to its customers.  We strive to work with the leading Medical OEM’s, becoming precision Molder choice and provide high quality components and assemblies thereby maintaining the quality as well as enhancing it.

 

 

 

INDUSTRIAL MARKET

GTS strives to be the manufacturing partner in Plastics of its customers in Industrial Market. We provide cost effective solutions providing consistent quality and service to a wide range of customers from small innovative companies to larger industry leaders.
